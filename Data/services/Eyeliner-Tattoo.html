ABOUT LASH LINER

Lash enhancement liner is tattooing the bottom line to top row of lashes, unlike the eyeliner that's tattooed on the top of the lash line to mimic eyeliner. The benefits of lash liner is it gives the illusion of thicker, darker lashes. It's subtle and natural, but makes a distinct difference.

You are not a good candidate for combo brows if you:

  • Have diabetes (consult your doctor).
  • Have any bleeding disorders.
  • Take blood-thinning medications (consult your GP and discontinue use if possible).
  • Had recently had Botox injections or fillers (wait at least 4 weeks).
  • Are pregnant or nursing women.
  • Have a history of keloids or hypertrophic scarring.
  • Have a viral infections or diseases.
  • Are going through chemotherapy.
  • Have a skin irritations or Psoriasis near the treated area.
  • Have tanned skin recently.
APPOINTMENT FIND AN ARTIST
4 WEEKS BEFORE -Avoid botox, skin exfoliants, laser or chemical peels.
2 MONTHS BEFORE - Discontinue any Retina-A, AHA's, or any activate skincare. Must not be taking any antibiotic medication.
2 WEEKS BEFORE - Avoid tweezing, waxing, retinol, brow laminations, and coloring your brows.
72 HOURS BEFORE -Avoid ALL blood thinners; Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Caffeine, Smoking, Alcohol, Vitamin E, Fish Oil, etc.
